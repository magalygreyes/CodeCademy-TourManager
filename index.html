How it works

The agent loop is what turns a chatbot into something that takes action. It decides, acts, reads the result, and decides again, until it has your answer.

STEP 01

You ask

In plain language. One sentence can cover weather and money at once.

STEP 02

It decides

The agent reads your question and chooses which tools it needs.

STEP 03

It acts

It calls live APIs for real weather and real exchange rates.

STEP 04

It answers

It blends the results into one clear, useful reply.
